The elements you need to consider when recording music.

Space

Studio

Studios are designed with sound in mind, so they are the ideal space to record in.

Home

If you do not have the resources, to book studio time, recording in the home is an option.

Other

Any space with great acousics can be considered for recording.

Microphones

Condenser

Condenser microphones are more sensitive and require an outside power source or battery.

Dynamic

Dynamic microphones are more durable than condenser microphones and often less expensive. They can be used on a wide variety of sources.

Outboard Gear

Compressors

Compression helps level the loudness throughout a piece. It makes the quiter parts louder and louder parts quiter for a more even listen.

Equalizers

Equalizers allow you to adjust the tone of the different elements in the recording.

Effects

Effects such as reverb, echo, and phaser can be added to the elements in a recording.
